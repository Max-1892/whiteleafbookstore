Whiteleaf Bookstore is based out of Baltimore, Maryland and has been serving the independent book market for 10 years. We pride ourselves on connecting book lovers to quality, independent books that haven't been given the attention they deserve. In addition to our large selection of books, we offer on-site readings, book signings, and IT support for your favorite e-reader.

Our entire book collection is available for browsing and purchasing via our online store.

Reach out to us via email or phone and pay us a visit next time you're in town!
